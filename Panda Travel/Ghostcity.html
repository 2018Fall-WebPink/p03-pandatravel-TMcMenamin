    • All About Ghost City Inn, Jerome, AZ:

    The Ghost Chity Inn, was first a house built for miners. It was build around 1890. It later became a Bed and Breakfast, a ashram, and a resturant in the prior years. It is currently a Bed and Breakfast that has some of the same architectural aspects such as bedboard ceilings, and floors. This Bed and Breakfast has a priavte bathroom in each bedroom, TV, ceiling fans, and air conditioning. This is a perfect halloween destination with wineries and tasting rooms right near by. There is a breathtaking view of the terraced red rocks of Sedona. At night the stars shine bright, and the night is dark and quite... Come visit Ghost City Inn a scary yet cozy destination for family friends and anyone you can fit in your suitcase!
